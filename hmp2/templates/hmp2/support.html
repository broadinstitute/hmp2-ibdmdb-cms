What is the format of provenance logs?

Provenance logs are generated by the AnADAMA2 workflow system during runtime and are plain-text files containing all steps executed, warnings, debug and error messages when present.

Does an NCBI BioProject exist that is associated with the data?

IBDMDB data is associated with the following BioProject: PRJNA398089
